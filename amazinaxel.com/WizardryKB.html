How to Join WizardryKB:

Self-hosted version:

To join the self-hosted version, launch Minecraft and click on the "Multiplayer" button. Then, click the "Add Server" button near the bottom. Enter the name: "WizardryKB" and the IP, "play.amazinaxel.com" and click Save. Now, just double click on the new server listing and join in on the fun!


Minehut Version:

Follow the same steps above, but enter the IP, "mc.minehut.com" and join the server. While you are in the lobby run the command, "/join WizardryKB" and you will be teleported right into WizardryKB Classic. There are a couple things to note about the Minehut version compared to the Self-hosted version:

  • You will only have access to WizardryKB Classic on Minehut. Playing on the self-hosted version allows you to play minigames, Wizardry Skywars, Wizardry Prototype, Wizardry Parkour and all upcoming gamemodes and minigames.
  • You will be able to experience new updates and features on the self-hosted version, because the Minehut version has an update delay of 1~3 days.
  • You will have access to extra features such as ranks, a level system, a Hub for chatting / cosmetics, and much more!
  • With the Minehut version, you will be able to play with more players and there are more player slots as compared to the self-hosted version.
  • The Minehut server system allows other players to join, allowing you to fight more people. With the self-hosted version, there usually is less people playing.
